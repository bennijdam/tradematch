## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit [www.tradematch.uk](https://www.tradematch.uk)
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
3. Choose your account type:
   - **Customer**: Post jobs and hire tradespeople
   - **Vendor**: Find jobs and grow your business
4. Enter your email and create a password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Verify your email address
6. Complete your profile

### Logging In

1. Click "Login" on the homepage
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

## For Customers

### Posting a Job

1. **From your dashboard**, click "Post a Job"
2. **Select service type** (e.g., Plumbing, Electrical, Painting)
3. **Enter job details**:
   - Title: Brief description (e.g., "Fix leaking kitchen tap")
   - Description: Detailed requirements
   - Postcode: Your location
   - Budget range: Minimum and maximum
   - Urgency: When you need it done
4. **Review and submit** your quote request
5. **Wait for bids** from verified tradespeople

### Reviewing Bids

1. Go to "My Jobs" in your dashboard
2. Click on a job to see bids
3. Review each vendor's:
   - Price quote
   - Experience and reviews
   - Availability
   - Message explaining their approach
4. Click "View Profile" to see full vendor details
5. Click "Accept Bid" to hire your chosen vendor

### Communicating with Vendors

Once you accept a bid:

1. **Message thread opens** automatically
2. Click "Messages" in your dashboard
3. Send messages, photos, or documents
4. Get real-time notifications when vendors reply

### Managing Payments

1. **Payment is held in escrow** until job completion
2. **Milestone payments**: Release funds as work progresses
3. **Final payment**: Release remaining balance when satisfied
4. **Payment methods**: Credit/Debit card, bank transfer

### Leaving Reviews

After job completion:

1. You'll receive a review request email
2. Rate the vendor (1-5 stars) on:
   - Quality of work
   - Communication
   - Value for money
   - Timeliness
3. Write a detailed review to help other customers

## For Vendors

### Setting Up Your Profile

1. **Complete business details**:
   - Company name
   - Services offered
   - Years of experience
   - Service areas
2. **Upload credentials**:
   - Trade certificates
   - Insurance documents
   - Company registration
3. **Add photos** of your work
4. **Set your preferences**:
   - Lead types you want
   - Service radius
   - Working hours

### Getting Verified

Verification builds trust:

1. **Identity verification**: Government ID check
2. **Trade registration**: Verify your trade body membership
3. **Insurance verification**: Public liability insurance
4. **Background check**: Basic criminal record check

### Buying Credits

Leads cost credits to access:

1. Go to "Credits" in your dashboard
2. Choose a package:
   - Starter: £50 for 10 credits
   - Pro: £200 for 50 credits
   - Unlimited: £500/month unlimited
3. Pay securely via Stripe
4. Credits never expire

### Bidding on Jobs

1. **Browse leads** in "Available Jobs"
2. **Filter by**: Location, service type, budget
3. **Review job details** carefully
4. **Submit your bid**:
   - Competitive price
   - Estimated duration
   - Your availability
   - Professional message
5. **Follow up** via messages if needed

### Accepting Leads (Premium)

Premium vendors get leads first:

1. **Lead arrives** via email and dashboard
2. **Review preview**: Service type, location, budget
3. **Click "Accept Lead"** to unlock full details
4. **Credits deducted** automatically
5. **Contact customer** immediately

## Account Management

### Profile Settings

1. Click your profile icon (top right)
2. Select "Settings"
3. Update:
   - Personal information
   - Contact details
   - Password
   - Notification preferences

### Notification Preferences

Choose how you want to be notified:

- **Email**: Daily digest, immediate, or none
- **SMS**: For urgent updates (vendors only)
- **Push**: Real-time browser notifications
- **In-app**: Dashboard notifications

### Privacy Settings

- **Profile visibility**: Public or private
- **Contact info**: Show phone/email or messages only
- **Review visibility**: Show all reviews

## Safety & Security

### For Customers

**Before hiring**:
- ✅ Check vendor verification badges
- ✅ Read all reviews
- ✅ Compare multiple quotes
- ✅ Verify insurance status

**During the job**:
- Never pay outside the platform
- Use milestone payments
- Keep communication on platform
- Don't share personal contact info until work starts

**Payment protection**:
- Funds held in escrow
- Release only when satisfied
- Dispute resolution available

### For Vendors

**Profile safety**:
- Verify your identity to build trust
- Keep insurance documents current
- Respond to leads within 24 hours
- Maintain high ratings

**Payment security**:
- Connect Stripe account securely
- Never ask for payment outside platform
- Funds release automatically after work approval

### Platform Security

- **JWT tokens** expire after 7 days
- **Rate limiting** prevents abuse
- **Input validation** on all forms
- **HTTPS encryption** for all data
- **Row-level security** in database

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is TradeMatch free to use?**
A: Yes for customers. Vendors pay for leads via credits.

**Q: What areas do you cover?**
A: Currently UK-wide, with focus on England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland.

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Email support@tradematch.uk or use the chat widget.

### Customer Questions

**Q: How many quotes should I get?**
A: We recommend 3-5 quotes for comparison.

**Q: What if I'm not happy with the work?**
A: Use our dispute resolution process. Funds are held in escrow until you approve.

**Q: Can I cancel a job?**
A: Yes, until you accept a bid. After acceptance, contact the vendor to discuss.

**Q: How long do vendors have to bid?**
A: Quotes typically receive bids within 24-48 hours.

### Vendor Questions

**Q: How much do leads cost?**
A: Between £5-25 per lead, depending on job value and competition.

**Q: Do credits expire?**
A: No, credits never expire.

**Q: What if I don't win any bids?**
A: Focus on response time, competitive pricing, and building your review profile.

**Q: Can I decline a lead?**
A: Yes, declining is free. Only accepting deducts credits.

**Q: How do I get verified?**
A: Upload your trade certificates and insurance in Settings > Verification.
