For a 6m¬≤ bathroom renovation in South London, budget ¬£5,000-¬£8,000.

Here's a detailed breakdown based on current 2025 London prices:

  • New bathroom suite (toilet, sink, shower/bath): ¬£800-¬£1,500
  • Tiling (walls and floor, mid-range tiles): ¬£1,200-¬£2,000
  • Lighting and electrics: ¬£400-¬£800
  • Plumbing and labor: ¬£2,000-¬£3,500
  • Miscellaneous (sealant, grout, extras): ¬£600-¬£1,200

London-specific costs: South London prices are typically 20-30% higher than the UK average due to higher labor costs, parking permits (¬£20-40/day), and congestion charges if applicable.

Money-saving tips without compromising quality:

  • Keep the bathroom layout the same - moving plumbing adds ¬£500-1,000
  • Choose mid-range tiles from Topps Tiles or CTD - good quality at fair prices
  • Standard white suite rather than designer brands (looks just as good)
  • Get 3-5 quotes - prices can vary significantly in London
  • Book in winter (Nov-Feb) when tradespeople are less busy

Important: Don't cheap out on waterproofing (tanking) - it'll cost 3x more to fix water damage later. Make sure your bathroom fitter is Part P registered for the electrical work.
