Frequently Asked Questions

Why don't I appear every time someone views a page?
To ensure fairness, we rotate all eligible vendors equally. Up to 3 vendors are shown per page view, and rotation ensures everyone gets equal opportunity. This prevents any single vendor from dominating local pages.
Why are only 3 vendors shown?
Research shows customers make better decisions when presented with a manageable number of options. Showing 3 high-quality vendors increases your chance of being contacted compared to showing 10+ vendors where you'd get lost in the crowd.
What happens when I run out of impressions?
Your profile will automatically pause on local service pages. You'll still appear in search results and customers can still find your profile directly, but you won't be shown in the rotated local page listings. You can top-up anytime to resume.
How is this fair compared to other platforms?
Unlike platforms where vendors bid against each other (driving costs up), TradeMatch uses fair rotation. Everyone with impressions available has equal chance to be shown. This makes costs predictable and prevents price wars between tradespeople.
Can I control which areas use more impressions?
Yes! You can pause impressions for specific areas at any time. This helps you focus your budget on high-performing areas while reducing spend in low-demand locations.
