Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I purchase credits?
Your credits are instantly added to your account and can be used immediately to access high-quality leads. You'll receive an email confirmation with your transaction details.
Can I get a refund?
Credits are non-refundable, but if a lead is invalid or the customer is unresponsive, we'll refund your credit cost. See our full refund policy for details.
Do credits expire?
No, your credits don't expire. Use them whenever you're ready at your own pace.
What's the difference between credit packages?
Larger packages offer better value per credit. For example, the 250-credit package is about 15% cheaper per credit than the starter package.
How are lead costs calculated?
Lead costs depend on job value, category, location, and quality. Premium leads cost more but have higher conversion rates and customer verification.
