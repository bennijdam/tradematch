    • Select your service type (plumbing, electrical, etc.)
    • Enter your postcode and job details
    • Describe what work needs doing (min 20 characters)
    • Add your contact details
    • Submit - it's completely free!

    You'll start receiving quotes from verified local tradespeople within hours.

  • Yes, 100% free! There are no hidden charges or fees for homeowners. You can:

    • Post unlimited jobs
    • Receive up to 5 quotes per job
    • Message tradespeople directly
    • View full profiles and reviews

    • Emergency jobs get faster responses

  • Yes! Every tradesperson on TradeMatch goes through our verification process:

    • ID Verification: Government-issued ID checked
    • Insurance Verification: Public liability insurance confirmed (minimum ¬£2M)
    • Qualifications: Gas Safe, NICEIC, and other certifications verified
    • Background Checks: Basic criminal record screening
    • Business Verification: Companies House registration confirmed

    Look for the "Verified" badge on profiles for extra peace of mind.

  • Payment is arranged directly between you and the tradesperson:

    • Agree on payment terms before work begins
    • Common structure: 10-20% deposit, balance on completion
    • Never pay 100% upfront
    • Get a written quote before starting
    • Keep receipts and invoices for warranty purposes

    For large projects (¬£5,000+), we recommend using our optional Payment Protection service for added security.

  • We're here to help if issues arise:

Frequently Asked Questions

üöÄ Getting Started

  • Signing up is quick and straightforward:

    • Visit tradematch.uk/vendors/signup
    • Enter your business details and trade
    • Upload ID and insurance documents
    • Add your service areas (postcodes)
    • Set up your payment method
    • Wait for verification (usually 24-48 hours)

    Once verified, you'll start receiving relevant job notifications immediately.

  • You'll need to provide:

    • Photo ID: Driving license or passport
    • Public Liability Insurance: Minimum ¬£2M coverage
    • Qualifications: Gas Safe, NICEIC, City & Guilds, etc.
    • Business Registration: Companies House or sole trader UTR
    • Bank Details: For receiving payments from customers

    All documents are securely stored and only used for verification.

  • No! Unlike other platforms, TradeMatch has no monthly fees. You only pay when you quote on jobs:

    • Per-lead pricing: ¬£3-¬£15 per quote (varies by job value)
    • No long-term contracts
    • No monthly minimums
    • No setup fees
    • Pause anytime if you're busy

    This means you're only spending money when you're actively pursuing work.

üí∞ Leads & Quoting

  • We send job notifications via:

    • Email: Instant alerts for new matching jobs
    • SMS: Optional text alerts (enable in settings)
    • Mobile App: Push notifications (iOS & Android)
    • Dashboard: View all available jobs anytime

    You can customize notification preferences in your account settings to only receive jobs you're interested in.

  • Lead prices are based on estimated job value:

    • Small jobs (¬£50-¬£200): ¬£3 per quote
    • Medium jobs (¬£200-¬£1,000): ¬£5-¬£8 per quote
    • Large jobs (¬£1,000-¬£5,000): ¬£10-¬£12 per quote
    • Major projects (¬£5,000+): ¬£12-¬£15 per quote

    The exact price is shown before you purchase each lead. You decide which jobs are worth quoting on.

  • Winning quotes typically include:

    • Fast Response: Quote within 1-2 hours if possible
    • Detailed Breakdown: Itemized costs for labor, materials, disposal
    • Clear Timeline: Start date and estimated completion
    • Professional Tone: Friendly but businesslike communication
    • References: Mention similar jobs you've completed
    • Payment Terms: Clear structure (deposit, milestones, final payment)

    Personalize each quote - don't use copy-paste templates!
