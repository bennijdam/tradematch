Our Story

How TradeMatch Was Born

In 2019, our founder Sarah Mitchell spent three frustrating weeks trying to find a reliable electrician to rewire her London flat. She called dozens of tradespeople who either never returned her calls, quoted wildly different prices, or simply didn't show up.

Sarah knew she wasn't alone. Millions of UK homeowners face the same problem every year: finding trustworthy, fairly-priced tradespeople is unnecessarily difficult, stressful, and time-consuming.

"There had to be a better way. A platform that worked for both homeowners and tradespeople. One built on transparency, fairness, and trust."

That's when TradeMatch was born. We set out to create a marketplace where homeowners could post jobs for free, receive multiple competitive quotes, and hire with complete confidence. At the same time, we wanted to give tradespeople a fair, affordable way to find quality leads without paying extortionate subscription fees.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 85,000 homeowners complete their projects, while supporting thousands of skilled tradespeople to grow their businesses. But we're just getting started.

Our vision is simple: to become the UK's most trusted platform for home improvement, where every homeowner can find the right tradesperson quickly, and every skilled professional can build a thriving business.
