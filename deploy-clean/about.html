About TradeMatch

We're on a mission to make finding trusted tradespeople simple, transparent, and reliable for everyone across the UK.

Our Story

TradeMatch was founded in 2024 with a simple vision: to bridge the gap between customers needing home improvements and skilled tradespeople looking for work. We saw firsthand how difficult it was for homeowners to find reliable professionals, and how talented tradespeople struggled to find consistent work.

Starting from a small office in London, we've grown into the UK's leading platform connecting customers with verified tradespeople. What makes us different is our commitment to transparency, quality, and building lasting relationships between customers and professionals.

Today, we're proud to have facilitated over 50,000 successful projects, saved customers millions in competitive bidding, and helped thousands of tradespeople grow their businesses.
