How We Calculate the Rankings

Our scoring model was designed to reward genuine performance and is deliberately impossible to game through advertising spend.

40%
Job Completion Rate
Percentage of accepted jobs completed without cancellation or abandonment. Weighted by job value and complexity.
30%
Verified Review Score
Only reviews from confirmed, invoiced jobs count. AI-flagged reviews are excluded pending manual verification.
20%
Response Time
Average time from lead notification to first contact with the homeowner. Measured in minutes from platform data.
10%
Dispute Rate
Proportion of jobs that resulted in a formal dispute. Penalises tradespeople with unresolved complaints.

Minimum threshold: A tradesperson must have completed at least 10 verified jobs in the calendar month to qualify for a ranking. This prevents low-volume traders appearing with inflated scores. Rankings are recalculated on the 1st of each month and use only data from the previous 30 days. Full methodology →
