The Problem We Found

The market was rigged against both sides.

Every major trades platform had quietly turned into a lead-extraction machine. Tradespeople paid upfront regardless of whether they won work. Homeowners got fake reviews, ghost leads, and zero payment protection. Nobody was protecting the people the platforms were supposed to serve.

Fake leads at scale
Platforms like Bark reported 50%+ ghost or unresponsive leads — vendors paid, got nothing.
Manipulated reviews
Five-day suppression windows. Bad reviews removed on request. No accountability.
Zero payment protection
Homeowners handed cash to strangers with no escrow, no protection, no recourse.
02
What We Built Instead

A marketplace with integrity at its core.

We started from first principles: what would the ideal trades marketplace look like if you genuinely cared about outcomes for both sides? Every feature on TradeMatch exists to answer a real problem we found in the existing market.

Phone-verified jobs only
Every job is verified before distribution. No ghost leads. No wasted spend.
Milestone escrow protection
Funds held until both parties confirm job completion. No cowboy builders. No unpaid tradespeople.
Reliability Scores, not just stars
Response rate, show-up rate, completion rate — real signal, not just reviews that can be bought.

What we're fixing

Four failures the market ignored.

The incumbent platforms had documented, public, systemic problems. Our founding team mapped every category of failure — and built a direct answer to each one.

See how we fixed it
Ghost & fake leads
Vendors on Bark.com reported paying for leads where homeowners never responded, numbers were fake, or the same lead was resold dozens of times.
Fixed: phone-verified jobs only
Suppressed & bought reviews
Checkatrade's 5-day suppression window allowed poor-performing vendors to flag and remove negative reviews before they published.
Fixed: immediate publish, tamper-proof
No payment protection
MyBuilder and Bark offer zero escrow. Homeowners hand money directly to tradespeople with no protection. Disputes are ignored.
Fixed: milestone escrow on every job
Pay-and-pray lead models
Platforms charging £700–1,200/year upfront — before a single job is won. Sole traders on thin margins locked into annual subs with no guaranteed ROI.
Fixed: pay-per-lead, no annual commitment

What we stand for

Four values that shape every decision.

Not written for a wall. Written because every feature, every hire, every product call at TradeMatch runs through these filters first.

01
Transparency above all
No suppressed reviews. No blind purchases. No hidden fees. If something is happening on this platform, both sides should be able to see it.
02
Protection is non-negotiable
Escrow. Lead quality guarantees. Verified credentials. A 48-hour dispute SLA. Protection isn't a feature — it's the foundation.
03
Fairness to both sides
Tradespeople and homeowners both matter equally. A platform that extracts from one side to serve the other eventually destroys itself. We designed for both.
04
Quality over quantity
Five vendors per lead, not fifty. Verified tradespeople only. Fewer, better leads. Fewer, better vendors. The whole platform is tuned for outcome quality, not volume metrics.
