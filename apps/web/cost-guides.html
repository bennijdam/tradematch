Our methodology

Cost data you can actually trust

Unlike price comparison sites that use outdated industry surveys, every figure in our guides comes directly from invoices and completed jobs on the TradeMatch platform.

Verified invoice data

Costs are drawn from completed job invoices submitted through our milestone escrow system — actual amounts paid, not estimated.

Postcode-level granularity

Regional breakdowns come from real job postcode data, not broad regional estimates.

Monthly refresh cycle

Data is recalculated every month as new jobs complete, ensuring guides reflect current market rates not pre-pandemic surveys.

Outlier filtering

Statistical outliers (emergency premium jobs, unusually complex work) are flagged separately so averages aren't distorted.
