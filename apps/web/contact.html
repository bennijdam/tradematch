Log into your dashboard and navigate to the relevant job. Click "Raise a Dispute" and describe the issue. Our trust team acknowledges within 4 hours and aims to resolve within 48 hours. You can also email disputes@tradematch.uk or use live chat for urgent cases.

You deposit funds which are held securely by TradeMatch and released at agreed milestones — typically 30% on start, 40% on practical completion, 30% on your sign-off. Neither party can access held funds without agreement, giving both sides full protection.

If a homeowner doesn't respond within 72 hours, you're eligible for a full lead credit — not a partial refund. Report it in your vendor dashboard and our team will review and credit your account within 24 hours.

During onboarding, upload your public liability insurance, trade registration (Gas Safe, NICEIC, NAPIT etc.), and photo ID. Our trust team verifies each document manually — usually within 24 hours. All credentials appear on your profile with last-verified dates.

No monthly fee is required to be on the platform. TradeMatch is pay-per-lead — you only pay when you receive a lead. Optional Pro (£29/mo) and Premium (£79/mo) subscriptions offer enhanced features but are never required.
