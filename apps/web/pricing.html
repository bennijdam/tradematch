How Lead Credits Work

Pay for connection,
not for browsing.

Each time you express interest in a job, one credit is used. You never pay to browse — only when you decide a lead is worth pursuing.

Browse for free See all available jobs in your area, trade, and price range without using any credits.
1 credit = 1 connection When you claim a lead, one credit is deducted. You then get the homeowner's contact details and job brief.
Credits refresh monthly Free plan: 3 credits/month. Pro: unlimited. Unused credits on Free roll over for 30 days.
Quality guarantee If a lead turns out to be fraudulent or duplicate, your credit is automatically refunded. No questions asked.

Is there really no contract on Pro?
Correct. Pro is a rolling monthly subscription. You can cancel at any time from your dashboard and your subscription ends at the next billing cycle. No penalties, no exit fees, no awkward calls.
What happens to my unused credits?
On the Free plan, unused credits roll over for up to 30 days. On Pro, there are no credits to worry about — you get unlimited lead access as part of your subscription.
How does the 14-day Pro trial work?
You get full Pro access for 14 days, completely free. No credit card required upfront. At day 14, you'll be prompted to add a payment method to continue. If you don't, you're automatically moved to the Free plan.
What if a lead is fraudulent or low quality?
We have a lead quality guarantee. If a homeowner is uncontactable, the job description is fraudulent, or you were matched to the wrong trade category, you can dispute the lead within 72 hours and receive a full credit refund.
Do homeowners pay anything?
No. Posting a job, receiving and comparing quotes, communicating with tradespeople, and using our escrow payment system is 100% free for homeowners. TradeMatch earns solely from tradespeople.
How does escrow payment protection work?
The homeowner deposits payment into a TradeMatch escrow account before work begins. Funds are only released to the tradesperson once the homeowner confirms the job is complete and they're satisfied. This protects both parties and is available on every job at no extra cost.
Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?
Yes. You can upgrade from Free to Pro instantly. Downgrading from Pro to Free takes effect at your next billing date. Enterprise customers should contact their account manager for changes.
