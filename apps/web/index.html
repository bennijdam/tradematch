Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

Is it really free to post a job?
Yes, completely. Posting a job on TradeMatch is free forever. Tradespeople pay us a small fee per lead they choose to quote on — that's how we keep the platform free for homeowners.
How are tradespeople verified?
Every tradesperson must pass government ID verification (biometric selfie match), provide proof of active public liability insurance, and submit any relevant trade accreditations (Gas Safe, NICEIC, etc). Our team manually reviews every application.
Why max 5 quotes? Other sites send more.
Because more isn't better — it's noise. Sites that blast your details to 30 contractors create a harassment experience. 5 focused, verified quotes give you real choice without the chaos. Every tradesperson who quotes actually wants the job.
What if I'm not happy with the work?
You don't approve payment until you're satisfied. If there's a genuine dispute, our resolution team investigates and can withhold funds. In serious cases, funds can be returned to you. Full details in our Escrow & Disputes policy.
How does escrow work exactly?
When you book a tradesperson, you deposit the agreed amount into our regulated escrow account. The tradesperson completes the work. You review and approve — then funds release to them within 24 hours. You're never pressured to approve prematurely.
How much does it cost to quote as a tradesperson?
Lead fees vary by trade and job size — typically £4–£18 per quote opportunity. There are no monthly subscriptions. You only pay when you actively choose to quote on a job. See full pricing on our Tradespeople Pricing page.
Do I need to create an account to post a job?
No. You can post a job as a guest. You'll only need to create an account when you want to review quotes and communicate with tradespeople — at which point your existing job is automatically linked.
Are the reviews genuine?
100%. Reviews on TradeMatch can only be left by homeowners who completed a verified, paid job through the platform. No anonymous reviews, no review manipulation. We scan for suspicious patterns and remove any that fail our checks.
