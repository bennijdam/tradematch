  • 348h countdown starts: A human Resolver is assigned and the vendor has 24h to submit their defence.

  • 5Resolver Lite Mode: For active on-site disputes, a simplified mobile view keeps focus on your response.

Common Questions

For Homeowners

When you select a quote and fund escrow, your payment goes directly into a Stripe-held, FCA-regulated account. TradeMatch cannot touch these funds without your explicit milestone approval. Each milestone is released only when you review the submitted evidence (photos + GPS logs) and tap "Approve." The final 20% is locked until a certificate or final sign-off is received. Every release generates an immutable reference number added to your Home Passport.

If a GPS-enabled vendor misses their arrival window without prior notice, an automatic alert is sent to TradeMatch. The vendor's Show-Up Score is penalised and escrow is paused. You can raise a dispute from the dashboard with one tap — the 48h resolution clock starts immediately and all funds remain frozen until the case is resolved. Consistently absent vendors are suspended from the platform.

Yes. Getting quotes, managing projects, using escrow, and raising disputes are all free for homeowners. The only cost is the TradeMatch Platform Fee, which is included in the vendor's quote total — it covers escrow management, dispute resolution, and the TradeMatch Guarantee. You never pay a subscription. Vendors fund the platform through lead purchases and plan subscriptions.

Before each job, a unique 4-digit PIN is generated and sent to your TradeMatch account. Your assigned tradesperson must enter this PIN via their app before they can unlock the job on the platform and access your address details. This prevents impersonation — if someone arrives claiming to be your booked tradesperson but can't enter the PIN, do not let them in and report it immediately from the app.

If you cannot reach an agreement, you can escalate to a formal Dispute, which triggers the 48h resolution process. The Resolver reviews all evidence and issues a binding decision on whether the funds should be released, partially released, or returned.

For Tradespeople

The AI engine matches jobs to vendors based on: trade category (parsed from job description), service area (your travel-time rings vs job postcode), active credentials (only live certifications count — expired certs exclude you from relevant jobs), Trust Score, and availability status. A 94% match score means the job is an excellent fit across all dimensions. Your AI matching priority increases as you complete your Credentials Vault and raise your Trust Score.

Once a homeowner approves a milestone, funds are released from escrow to your TradeMatch Wallet within minutes via Stripe Connect. From your Wallet, you can:

  • Instant payout — transfer to your bank immediately (small fee may apply)
  • Weekly payout — automated batch transfer every Monday
  • Use as platform credit — spend your wallet balance on lead purchases

Configure your payout preference in Settings → Financial → Escrow & Wallet.

TradeMatch monitors your PLI expiry date automatically. You'll receive:

  • 28-day urgent alert shown on your Credentials Vault and dashboard
