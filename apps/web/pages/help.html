How Can We Help?

TradeMatch is the UK's leading platform connecting customers who need home improvements with verified, skilled tradespeople. We make it easy to post quotes, receive competitive bids, and hire trusted professionals for your projects.
Yes! For customers, posting quotes and receiving bids is completely free. For tradespeople, creating an account and browsing quotes is free. We only charge a small commission on completed projects.
All tradespeople go through our verification process including ID checks, insurance validation, and qualification verification. We also monitor customer reviews and ratings to maintain high standards.
We cover the entire United Kingdom with verified tradespeople in every major city and region. Simply enter your postcode when posting a quote to find local professionals.
Click "Get Free Quotes" on the homepage, fill out our simple 4-step form describing your project, budget, and timeline. Your quote will be visible to verified tradespeople in your area within minutes.
Most customers receive 3-5 competitive bids within 24 hours. The number depends on your project type, budget, and location. You're free to accept any bid that meets your needs.
Compare bids based on price, timeline, tradesperson rating, years of experience, and customer reviews. You can message tradespeople directly to ask questions before making your decision.
Contact our support team immediately. We'll work with both parties to resolve any issues. If necessary, we can mediate disputes and, in serious cases, remove tradespeople from our platform.
Click "Sign Up as Tradesperson," complete our registration form with your company details and services, then go through our verification process. Once approved, you can start browsing and bidding on quotes immediately.
Browse available quotes in your area, click on projects that match your skills, review the requirements, then submit your bid with your price, timeline, and a message explaining why you're the best choice.
We charge a 10% commission on completed projects paid through our platform. There are no monthly fees, subscription costs, or charges for browsing and bidding on quotes.
Deliver excellent work, communicate clearly, arrive on time, and exceed customer expectations. After completing projects, politely ask satisfied customers to leave a review. High ratings lead to more job opportunities.
Customers and tradespeople arrange payment terms directly. We recommend using our secure payment system for protection, but direct payment is also allowed. Always get a written contract before starting work.
Payment terms are agreed between you and the tradesperson. Common arrangements include deposits, stage payments, or payment upon completion. Never pay the full amount upfront without a proper contract.
TradeMatch doesn't hold or process payments, so refunds are arranged directly between customers and tradespeople. If you have a payment dispute, contact our support team for mediation assistance.
VAT rules depend on the tradesperson's registration status. VAT-registered tradespeople will include VAT in their quotes. Always request an itemized quote showing all costs including VAT if applicable.
