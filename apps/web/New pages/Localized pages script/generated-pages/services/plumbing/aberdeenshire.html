Common questions about
Plumbing in Aberdeenshire

How much does Plumbing cost in Aberdeenshire?
Plumbing in Aberdeenshire typically costs between £60 and £160 for the first hour, including call-out. Subsequent hours usually run £40-£90. Emergency or out-of-hours work commands a premium - expect to pay 50-80% more than standard rates. Always confirm whether the quote includes VAT and materials.
Are all Plumbing on TradeMatch verified?
Yes — every tradesperson on TradeMatch is manually ID verified, DBS checked, and must hold valid public liability insurance before they can respond to jobs. We don't allow self-registration.
How long will it take to get quotes?
Most Aberdeenshire homeowners receive their first quote within a few hours. We guarantee responses within 48 hours, and typically you'll have 3–5 quotes to compare by the next day.
Is it really free to post a job?
Yes, completely free for homeowners. No hidden fees, no commission, no subscription. TradeMatch charges a small platform fee to the tradespeople, not to you.
How does the escrow payment work?
When you agree to a quote, you pay into a secure escrow account. The tradesperson begins work knowing funds are committed. Once you confirm the job is complete and you're satisfied, the funds are released. If there's a dispute, our team mediates.
What if I'm not happy with the work?
Don't release the escrow payment. Contact our resolution team and we'll mediate with the tradesperson to get the issue fixed. If unresolved, we invoke our TradeMatch Guarantee and arrange alternative works at no extra cost.
