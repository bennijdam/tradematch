Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using TradeMatch

How long does it take to receive quotes? +

Most jobs receive their first quote within a few hours of posting. You'll typically have 3-5 quotes within 24 hours. Response times can vary based on the type of work and your location, but our tradespeople are incentivized to respond quickly.

Is TradeMatch really free for customers? +

Yes, absolutely. There are no fees to post a job, receive quotes, or message tradespeople. You only pay the tradesperson you hire for the work they do. We make money by charging tradespeople a small fee when they choose to quote on your job.

Are the tradespeople verified? +

Yes. All tradespeople go through ID verification and business checks before they can quote. We also verify insurance and qualifications where applicable. Plus, you can read genuine reviews from previous customers to help you choose.

What if I don't like any of the quotes? +

No problem at all. There's zero obligation to hire anyone. You can decline all quotes, ask for revisions, or simply let them expire. Your job posting stays active for 7 days, and you may receive additional quotes during that time.

How do I know which tradesperson to choose? +

Compare quotes side-by-side, read verified customer reviews, view photos of their past work, and check their response times. You can also message them directly to ask questions. Look for a combination of competitive pricing, good reviews, and clear communication.

What types of jobs can I post? +

Almost any home improvement or trade work! From emergency repairs to full renovations. We cover plumbing, electrical, building, carpentry, painting, roofing, landscaping, and 40+ other categories. If you're not sure, just post it ‚Äì we'll match you with the right professionals.

How do payments work? +

You arrange payment directly with your chosen tradesperson. Most accept bank transfer, cash, or card. For larger projects, we offer optional payment protection through our escrow service for an extra layer of security (small fee applies).

What if something goes wrong with the work? +

First, try to resolve it directly with the tradesperson ‚Äì most issues are simple misunderstandings. If that doesn't work, contact our support team. We'll mediate disputes and can remove tradespeople who consistently fail to meet standards. All our tradespeople must have insurance.
